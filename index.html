🛡️
Safety Scores
Routes are scored 0–96% based on road type, lighting data, and proximity to police stations and hospitals from OpenStreetMap.
🌙
Night Mode
Tap 🌙 to enable night scoring — unlit roads get heavily penalised. Auto-enables after 8 PM.
🚶
Live Navigation
After finding routes, tap 🚶 Walk on any route card to start live GPS navigation with turn-by-turn directions.
🚨
SOS Button
The red SOS button dials emergency services and shares your location. Tap anytime for immediate help.
